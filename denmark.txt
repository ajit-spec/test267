Denmark is a Scandinavian country known for its high quality of life and social welfare system.
The capital city Copenhagen is famous for its cycling culture and sustainable urban planning.
The country consists of the Jutland Peninsula and 443 named islands, with Copenhagen as its capital.
Danish cuisine is known for smørrebrød (open-faced sandwiches) and pastries like the Danish.
The country has a rich Viking history dating back to the 8th-11th centuries.
